Dataglyphs: Inline Data Visualization

Dataglyphs are tiny visualizations embedded directly in the flow of text. They provide immediate visual context without disrupting reading.

Basic Example

Stock prices have been fluctuating over the past week, with trading volumes showing similar volatility.

Styling with Text

Revenue is trending upward , while costs are decreasing , and customer satisfaction remains consistent .

Different Widths

Comparing narrow and wide dataglyphs with the same data.

Font Weight Integration

Regular text with and bold text with and light text with all adapt to match the text weight.

Implementation

<p> Sales have been trending <data-glyph data="3,6,2,7,5,9,4" width="3em"></data-glyph> over the past week. </p>

How It Works

Dataglyphs automatically derive their height, color, and baseline from the surrounding text. They normalize the data range automatically to fit within the visual space.
